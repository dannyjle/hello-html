Getting Lost in Another's World

Photo by Gracia Dharma on Unsplash

The art of animation has always been something that drew out wonder, curiosity, and most importantly joy in me. I remember waking up an hour earlier every morning so that I could catch an episode of Dragon Ball Z and Sailor Moon. It was my favorite pastime before I even knew people had pastimes.

Eventually, I realized most people my age no longer watched animated cartoons, (let alone anime,) so I hid this form of escapism from the rest of the world. I never gave up on it thought and my passion for the art form only grew as I did. A book can transport you to another world, but it still relies a lot on your imagination. The animated content you consume can immerse you into someone elses world and your only real job is to enjoy the dive.

Some of my favorite animations/animes includes:

  • Digimon
  • Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood
  • Avatar: The Last Airbender and The Legend of Korra
  • Steven Universe
